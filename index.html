My Projects

This Web Application was made with the purpose of providing an affordable alternative of Zomato to the smaller food venders that lie within a specified radius of educational institutions. Such interested vendors can register on the app and provide their menu. The target audience for this app are students who because of budget constraints do not want to order from fancy cafes. Since this operates within a small area, the consession on each order would be cut off drastically, giving a greater incentive for such vendors to opt in. It used HTML, CSS and basics of JavaScript. It also features an inbuilt functioning cart.
